About Us

Our Founder

Abdoulie Balisa

Abdoulie Balisa founded QuantumInsights with a vision to transform how businesses leverage data. With a background in data science and statistics, and experience working with global tech companies, Abdoulie returned to The Gambia determined to build a consultancy that would bridge the data literacy gap across the country and beyond.

His journey began when he identified that many African businesses were collecting vast amounts of data but lacked the expertise to transform it into actionable insights. This realization led to the creation of QuantumInsights, which has since grown from a one-person operation to a team of data professionals serving clients across The Gambia and Africa at large.
