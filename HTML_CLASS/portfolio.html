About Me

My Journey

Born and raised in Gambia, my passion for data science and technology began when I first discovered how data could be used to solve real-world problems in my community. This curiosity led me to pursue higher education at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST) in Ghana, where I was honored to be selected as a Mastercard Foundation Scholar.

My academic journey has been complemented by hands-on experience in data analysis, machine learning, and cybersecurity. I believe in the power of data to drive innovation and create positive change, especially in emerging economies across Africa.

Today, I focus on developing data-driven solutions that address critical challenges in various sectors, from healthcare to finance, always with an eye toward security, scalability, and social impact.
