African Perspectives

Our Academic Programs

Primary School

  • African storytelling
  • Basic STEM

Secondary School

Rigorous academic preparation with focus on African solutions to global challenges.

  • Ages 12-18
  • African history & philosophy
  • Advanced STEM

Leadership Institute

Developing Africa's next generation of ethical leaders and change-makers.

  • Ages 16-19
  • Social entrepreneurship
  • Pan-African studies
